About Us

Pioneer Diagnostic Center (PDC) was established in 2006 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia and since then has been one of the leading diagnostic imaging centers in the country. Our bases of operations are located at four branches:

● Our Main branch is on Bole Road Behind Alem Cinema,
● At Gotera Condominium Inside Hallelujah Hospital,
● Around Black Lion Hospital near Radio Fana Broadcasting Corporation
● On the way to the Megenagna area at Lancet Super Specialized Medical and Surgical Center.

The PDC offers patients a full array of imaging services in a comfortable outpatient setting. Due to our superb care and attention to our patients both professionally, and humanistically, we have earned a Green Certificate from the Ministry of Health for our ‘Quality and Compassionate Patient Care’.

The PDC has been providing diagnostic imaging to physicians, technologists, and clients by blending advanced technology, extensive clinical expertise, and patient care. The PDC provides 128 Slice CT Scans, 1.5 Tesla MRI, Echocardiography, Digital X-rays, 4D Ultrasound, Digital 3D Mammography and Ambulance Services 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
All our radiologists, technologists, and nurses are certified by the Ministry of Health and by the Radiation Authority of Ethiopia. Our radiologists are certified in different sub-specialty such as Neuroradiology, Body Imaging, Cardiothoracic and Musculoskeletal.
The PDC also provides a 50% discount service every day after midnight and on Sunday’s. PDC distinctive features are its 1.5T MRI machine, 24 hours diagnostic services, quality images, PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication System), Sub-specialty reading, free ambulance service and our home environment make us preferred by all our customers. We provide transportation services to CT and MRI clients using our four Ambulance vehicles for free.
