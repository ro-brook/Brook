DR ASSEFA GETACHEW, MD

Associate Professor of Radiology

PROFILE:

Dr Assefa Getachew is an Associate Professor of Radiology in Addis Ababa University, Department of Radiology- College of Health Sciences – TAH. He is the president of the RADIOLOGICALal Society of Ethiopia. He has subspecialized in Body Imaging and has consultancy experience in the field of Radiology. His areas of clinical interest include all aspects of RADIOLOGICALal consultancy and a subspecialty in Body Imaging.
